BUTTER CHICKEN

DESCRIPTION

Similar to Chicken Tikka Masala, Butter Chicken is one of the most popular curries at any Indian restaurant around the world. Aromatic, golden chicken pieces in an incredible, creamy curry sauce, this Butter Chicken recipe is one of the best you will try!

Not only is this butter chicken incredibly delicious, but it is also very easy to make in your very own kitchen. Once youâ€™ve mastered this recipe, paying top dollar for take out will be a thing of the past

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Masala
  • Curd
  • Cashews

STEPS

  1. Marinate the chicken using masala and curd
  2. Take a pan and put oil on it and keep it in stove with medium heat
  3. Put your onions in the pan after cutting it
  4. After the onions get a brown texture keep all the masala, chopped tomatoes, garlic and Cashews
  5. After they are cooked take them out and mix them in a mixer
  6. Put your marinated chicken in the pan and fry it
  7. Take a pan again and fry the marinated chicken
  8. Now take a pan again and heat the mixed sauce
  9. Keep your chicken in that sauce and add some butter and cream
